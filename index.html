Clarify Your Doubts

What is an AMFI Registered Mutual Fund Distributor?
An AMFI Registered Mutual Fund Distributor (MFD) is authorized by the Association of Mutual Funds in India to distribute mutual fund schemes to investors. As a registered MFD (ARN-247468), Wealth Acumen assists you in choosing appropriate schemes, completing KYC formalities, facilitating transactions, and tracking your portfolio systematically.
How does the Channel Partnership with Angel One benefit me?
Our official Channel Partnership with Angel One grants you access to one of India's leading digital trading interfaces. You enjoy cutting-edge stock charting tools, easy Demat account integration, instant equity/IPO/bond transactions, and research recommendations, all backed by Wealth Acumen's localized support.
What is a Demat-Free Mutual Fund Investment account?
Demat-free mutual fund investing allows you to purchase mutual fund units in statement-of-account (SOA) form rather than holding them in a traditional Demat depository. This simplifies transactions, bypasses depository annual maintenance charges (AMC), and makes account management easier for long-term goal-based SIPs.
Are there any hidden advisory charges or signup fees?
No, Wealth Acumen believes in absolute transparency. We do not charge upfront consultation fees or hidden subscription costs. As mutual fund distributors, we receive commissions directly from AMC fund providers on regular mutual fund plans, which are fully disclosed.
How can I track my investment portfolio online?
Once you sign up through our Angel One portal or Demat-Free dashboard, you will be given individual secure login credentials. You can view, track, analyze, and manage your entire holdings 24/7 on both desktop and Android/iOS smartphones.
How do I start my first Systematic Investment Plan (SIP)?
Starting is easy! You can use our "DIY (Do It Yourself)" tab to register on our online platform, or drop us a call back request in our Contact section. We will help you complete your KYC online, link your bank account safely, and schedule your automated monthly SIP investments.
